My career so far!

My role consisted mainly of negotiating rental agreements between Shipowners and Charterers based on their respective needs. The other part for my job was to maintain our database regarding ships specifications. And of course some socialising with colleagues/clients(past/present/futur)

I came back from Asia and decided to make a transition in the IT industry and got a job as a Technical Specialist for Apple in Bromley.
